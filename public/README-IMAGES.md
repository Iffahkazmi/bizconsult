# Required Images for Launch

Before launching, create these images:

## 1. Favicon (public/favicon.ico)
- Size: 32x32 pixels
- Format: ICO
- Contains: Logo or "BC" initials

## 2. Apple Touch Icon (public/apple-touch-icon.png)
- Size: 180x180 pixels
- Format: PNG
- Contains: App logo

## 3. Open Graph Image (public/og-image.png)
- Size: 1200x630 pixels
- Format: PNG
- Contains: 
  - "BizConsult AI" title
  - Tagline: "Validate Your Business Idea with AI"
  - Professional background
  - Your logo

## Tools to Create:
- Canva (free): https://canva.com
- Figma (free): https://figma.com
- Favicon Generator: https://favicon.io

## Quick Tip:
Use your brand colors (blue #2563eb from Tailwind)
